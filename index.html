The International Science Youth Forum (ISYF) is a prestigious science enrichment programme for top research students from all over the globe, hosted by Hwa Chong Institution. This year, the ISYF Student Organising Committee comprises all the members of the school's Students' Science Research Club (SSRC). The delegates of ISYF will gain the opportunity to participate in a multitude of exciting and meaningful activities, which include intellectual discourse with eminent scientists, technopreneurs, medical professionals and government officers who utilise technology and big data to make decisions, as well as networking with peers through collaborative projects and cultural sharings.

ISYF 2023 will take place from 15 to 19 January 2023.

Dear Student and Teacher Delegates,

Welcome to the 15th International Science Youth Forum (ISYF) @ Singapore, held from 15 to 19 January 2023!

After the past two iterations of ISYFdigital resulting from the COVID-19 Pandemic and the unprecedented challenges it has brought upon humanity, we are delighted to be able to invite you physically to Singapore this year once again. Our forum's purpose of facilitating intellectual discourse and cultural exchange among young scientific talents is especially paramount, given humankind's present opportunity to imagine our world anew amidst its present recovery. How can we, as catalysts for change, better drive humankind's progress anchored on the scientific method and discipline?

In the spirit of redefining boundaries and galvanising transformation, ISYF 2023's theme of Reconnecting, Reimagining: Looking Ahead to a Better World inspires our scientifically-talented youth delegates to lead the charge into a better future, united by common goals, shared knowledge, and global solidarity.

The Organising Team of the 15th ISYF looks forward to meeting you, and we hope that you will have a fruitful and memorable time in Singapore!
